Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I post a project?
Posting a project is completely free and easy. Click "Post Project" in the navigation, fill out the project details including description, budget, timeline, and requirements. Once submitted, qualified voice talent will start applying within hours.
How much does it cost to use VoicecastingPro?
For clients, VoicecastingPro is completely free. You can post unlimited projects, browse talent, and communicate directly with voice artists at no cost. Voice talent pay $35/month for professional membership to access projects and premium features.
How do I choose the right voice talent?
Listen to demo reels, read client reviews, check their experience in your industry, and consider their communication style. Don't hesitate to ask questions about their process, equipment, and availability before making a decision.
What if I'm not satisfied with the final recording?
Most professional voice talent include reasonable revisions in their project price. Communicate specific feedback clearly, and work with the talent to achieve your desired result. If issues persist, contact our support team for assistance.
How long does it typically take to receive a voice over?
Delivery times vary based on project length and complexity. Simple commercial spots may be delivered within 24-48 hours, while longer projects like audiobooks or e-learning courses may take several days to weeks. Always discuss timeline expectations upfront.
